Unitary Fund - Member of Technical Staff (Part-time)

Unitary Fund is a non-profit research group dedicated to accelerating the development of a quantum technology industry that benefits the most people. We run a grant program to support the development of new open source quantum software, and we fund our own research on key projects for the ecosystem.

We’re hiring Members of Technical Staff to work on our first of these key projects: the development of an open source toolchain for error-mitigated quantum programming. You will develop software and perform research as part of the US Department of Energy funded TEAM collaboration between Unitary Fund, Stanford, Lawrence Livermore National Lab, Johns Hopkins Applied Physics Lab, the University of Chicago, and the University of Maryland.

Through this project you’ll be at the center of the emerging field of quantum computing and working to develop new and critical components to accelerate it.

This is a part-time role of (40-60% FTE), and we are open to remote-work candidates.

About You:

  • 2+ years of software engineering work experience (not necessarily quantum related) OR 2+ years of research experience in quantum information such as in a PhD program
  • Track record of learning/building with quantum computing packages and software (e.g. any of these open source projects)
  • Excited to make quantum programming better, easier, and more accessible

Applications are closed at this time.
